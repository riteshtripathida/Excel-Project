# Vrinda Store Annual Sales Analysis Dashboard – Excel Project

## Project Objective:
To analyze Vrinda Store's annual sales data using Microsoft Excel and build an interactive dashboard to uncover sales trends, customer demographics, and business opportunities, enabling informed decision-making and sales growth.
## Datasheet used:
https://github.com/riteshtripathida/Excel-Project/blob/main/Vrinda%20Store%20Annual%20Report.xlsx

## Questions(KPIs):
-Compare the sales and orders using Single chart?
-Which month got the highest sales and orders ?
-Who Purchased more Men or Women ?
-List Top 5 states contributing to the sales ?
-Which channel is contributing to maximam sales ?
-Relations between age and gender based on number ?

## Process:
-Collected and cleaned annual sales data in Excel.
-Performed data analysis using Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts.
-Created interactive dashboards with Slicers for dynamic filtering.
-Analyzed sales trends, customer demographics, order status, top states, and sales channels.
-Generated business insights and recommended strategies to improve sales performance.

## Tools used:
Microsoft Excel | Pivot Tables | Pivot Charts | Slicers | Data Cleaning | Data Visualization | Business Analysis

## Dashboard:

## Final Conclusion:
The analysis of Vrinda Store's annual sales data revealed that women aged 30–49 years are the primary customers, contributing the highest share of sales. Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh emerged as the top-performing states, while Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra were the leading sales channels. By targeting this customer segment through personalized promotions and marketing campaigns, Vrinda Store can effectively enhance customer engagement and drive higher sales growth.
